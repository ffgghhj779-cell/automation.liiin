## 🔑 How to Extract Cookie Safely

**IMPORTANT: Only do this AFTER warm-up period**

### **Step 1: Log In Normally**
1. Use **regular browser** (Chrome/Firefox/Edge - NOT incognito)
2. Go to `linkedin.com`
3. Log in with email/password
4. Browse normally for 2-3 minutes

### **Step 2: Extract Cookie**
1. Press **F12** (Developer Tools)
2. Click **Application** tab (Chrome) or **Storage** (Firefox)
3. Expand **Cookies** section
4. Click `https://www.linkedin.com`
5. Find cookie named `li_at`
6. **Double-click the VALUE** to select
7. **Right-click → Copy**
8. Paste into secure notepad/password manager

### **Step 3: Verify Cookie**
1. Cookie should start with `AQEDAT`
2. Should be 100+ characters long
3. No spaces or special formatting

### **Step 4: Keep Browser Open**
- Don't log out
- Don't close browser yet
- Leave it running in background

**Why?** Keeps session active, looks more natural when automation starts.

## 🔍 What "Natural Behavior" Looks Like

### **Good Patterns:**
✅ Login → Scroll feed → Read post → Like → Scroll more → Read → Comment → Scroll → Logout  
✅ 20-30 seconds between actions  
✅ Reading time varies (some posts 10s, some 60s+)  
✅ Not every post is liked/commented  
✅ Mouse movements, scrolling up/down  
✅ Some days more active, some less  

### **Bot Patterns (Avoid):**
❌ Login → Like 20 posts in 2 minutes → Logout  
❌ Exact same timing every day (9:00 AM sharp)  
❌ Like/comment on EVERY post  
❌ No reading time (instant likes)  
❌ No variation in behavior  
❌ No mouse movements (keyboard only)

## ⚠️ Warning Signs to Watch For

**During warm-up, if you see:**

❌ **CAPTCHA during manual use**
- STOP immediately
- Account may have issues
- Don't proceed to automation

❌ **"Unusual activity" message**
- Slow down manual activity

- Session expired (normal)
- Or suspicious activity detected (bad)
- Log back in manually
